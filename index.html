FAQ

Questions before checking stock.

Short answers for overseas buyers before contacting CLAY TCG.

Do you ship worldwide?

We support many international destinations where FedEx or DHL service is available. Please confirm your delivery country before ordering.

Which carriers do you use?

International shipments are handled through FedEx and DHL where service is available.

How can I check current stock and prices?

Open Discord to view current stock, check prices, and ask our team in Japan.

How do I request a quotation?

Send the product name, quantity, and delivery country through Discord. Our team will confirm availability and shipping cost.

Do I need to buy after joining Discord?

No. Joining Discord is free and does not require a purchase.

Are import duties and taxes included?

Import duties, customs fees, GST, VAT, and local taxes may apply and are generally the buyer's responsibility unless agreed otherwise.

How is shipping cost calculated?

Shipping cost depends on quantity, weight, destination, carton size, and carrier conditions. It is confirmed before ordering.
