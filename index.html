FAQ

Questions buyers usually ask before joining.

These answers are written for overseas buyers who want to understand CLAY before contacting us through Discord.

Do I need to place an order to join Discord?

No. You can join to ask about stock, pricing, shipping, and upcoming releases before placing an order.

Do you sell to individual collectors?

Yes. CLAY supports individual collectors as well as shops, resellers, and group buyers.

Do you support card shops and resellers?

Yes. Shops and resellers can ask about quantity, sealed product availability, shipping conditions, and repeat order needs.

Which countries do you ship to?

We support many countries where FedEx or DHL service is available, including the United States, Canada, Australia, Hong Kong, the United Kingdom, Europe, Singapore, and more.

Which carriers do you use?

International shipments are handled through FedEx and DHL where service is available.

How can I check current stock and prices?

Please join Discord. Stock, pricing, and product availability can change quickly, so Discord is the current contact point.

How are shipping costs calculated?

Shipping costs depend on destination, package weight, carton size, quantity, and carrier conditions. Please request a quote before ordering.

How quickly do you dispatch?

Standard confirmed orders are usually dispatched same day or next day. Pre-orders, special sourcing, larger orders, and carrier schedules may require more time.

Are import duties or taxes included?

Import duties, customs fees, GST, VAT, and local taxes vary by country and are generally the buyer's responsibility unless specifically agreed otherwise.

Can I ask questions before ordering?

Yes. Discord is designed for questions about products, shipping, timing, and order conditions before purchase.

How can I verify the company?

Company name, Japanese name, corporate number, address, representatives, and business scope are listed in the Company Information section below.

What condition information is provided?

Condition and product details should be confirmed during the quote discussion, especially for higher-value or special requests.
